# AI Automation Portfolio

Welcome to my AI Automation Portfolio.

This repository presents a professional overview of my experience designing, leading, and maintaining AI powered automation systems for international business operations. ## About me

I am an Environmental Engineer who redirected my analytical and technical background toward AI automation, workflow optimization, and process improvement. My recent professional experience has focused on leading automation projects, mainly in n8n, while coordinating an automation specialist and supporting international companies across the USA, Europe, and Asia.

My work has included AI agents, workflow architecture, API integrations, CRM synchronization, lead generation systems, web scraping, automated reporting, data processing pipelines, calendar automation, knowledge base ingestion, and document generation systems.

## Selected project examples

### LinkedIn outreach automation system

An AI powered outreach ecosystem that converts target audience criteria into prospect searches, extracts lead data, generates company analysis, creates personalized outreach messages, and tracks campaign outputs.

### Calendar automation agent

A workflow system for interpreting scheduling requests, creating meetings, managing attendees, and coordinating calendar actions through connected productivity tools.

### RAG knowledge ingestion system

A document and media ingestion pipeline that retrieves files, processes content, creates embeddings, stores information in Pinecone, and supports knowledge based AI assistants.

### Voice assistant and professor bot

A Telegram based assistant capable of receiving voice or text inputs, converting voice messages into text, retrieving context from a vector database, and generating useful responses.

### AI Opportunity Audit generator

A business audit workflow that receives website inputs, extracts public content, analyzes AI implementation opportunities, and generates structured recommendations.

### Competitive Edge Blueprint

A strategic automation workflow that analyzes company websites, competitors, market signals, opportunity gaps, TAM related assumptions, and prioritized actions.

### Brand Positioning Audit and GTM Blueprint

A report generation system that combines website analysis, social media inputs, competitor benchmarking, and AI strategy generation to produce brand positioning and go to market insights.

### ScripCast AI Video Content Automation System

An AI content operations workflow that generates video monologues, creates captions, produces avatar based videos, routes content by social platform, publishes through connected social media tools, and tracks final outputs in Google Sheets.

## My approach

My approach starts by understanding the business process and identifying repetitive, manual, or fragmented tasks. From there, I design workflows that connect the right platforms, use AI only where it adds value, and prioritize reliability, scalability, documentation, and maintainability.

• Process discovery  
• Workflow architecture  
• Data mapping
