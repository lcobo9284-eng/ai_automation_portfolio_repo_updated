# Competitive Edge Blueprint

## Project summary

Built a strategic automation workflow for generating competitive intelligence reports, opportunity gap analysis, prioritized actions, market insights, and business recommendations from company and competitor inputs.

## Business challenge

Competitive research is often time consuming and inconsistent when completed manually. The objective was to create a repeatable system that could gather website data, analyze competitive positioning, and produce structured business strategy outputs.

## Solution approach

The workflow fetched website content, extracted structured information, used AI agents to generate strategic analysis, organized findings into a document ready payload, and passed the output to Make.com for report creation.

## Core capabilities

• Website content retrieval  
• HTML data extraction  
• AI research and analysis  
• Competitive positioning assessment  
• Opportunity gap analysis  
• Prioritized action generation  
• TAM related assumptions and business strategy sections  
• Report document generation  

## Tools used
