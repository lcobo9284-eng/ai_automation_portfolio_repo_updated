# Brand Positioning Audit and GTM Blueprint

## Project summary

Developed an AI powered report generation system for brand positioning, go to market analysis, website review, social media review, competitor benchmarking, and strategic recommendations.

## Business challenge

Brand and go to market research requires collecting information from websites, social channels, competitor inputs, and business context. Manual reporting can be slow, inconsistent, and difficult to scale.

## Solution approach

The workflow collected client and competitor inputs, extracted website content, processed social media related information, grouped data by platform, generated AI based insights, and produced structured report outputs for document and presentation generation.

## Core capabilities

• Intake form for company and competitor data  
• Website analysis  
• Social media data organization  
• Competitor benchmarking  
• Brand positioning analysis  
• GTM strategy recommendations  
• Document and presentation generation  
• Email delivery and internal notifications
