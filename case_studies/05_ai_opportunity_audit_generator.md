# AI Opportunity Audit Generator

## Project summary

Developed an AI powered audit workflow that receives a company website and optional business context, analyzes the public digital presence, and generates an AI opportunity report with recommended automation use cases.

## Business challenge

Businesses often want to understand where AI can create operational value but lack a structured way to identify opportunities across sales, marketing, operations, customer experience, and reporting.

## Solution approach

The workflow collected website inputs through a form, fetched website content, extracted relevant HTML information, analyzed the business context with AI, generated recommendations, created a document through Make.com, and optionally delivered outputs through email or internal notification channels.

## Core capabilities

• Form based intake  
• Website content extraction  
• HTML data parsing  
• AI opportunity analysis  
• Report generation  
• Make.com document handoff  
• Slack and Google Sheets tracking  
• Optional email delivery
